# Working Narrative

## High Level Story Beats

* A free crew is give a contract to traffic rare biological and mineral samples from the new world back to X shipping vessels  
  * What they don’t know is that there is a rare metaphysical element called Vitaelum known and highly sought after by all major political factions. It has been found in Conway’s Web on certain worlds with particularly high density of life forms.   
* On their journey they meet the people who want to create worlds of their own governance  
  * The crew is faced with the choice to ally with the settlers of the free world or bring the fist of civilization  
  * Whole worlds are organized around the idea of starting again  
  * Worlds are lush with abundant life  
  * By removing the Vitaelum, the worlds start to die  
  * If the players side with the free settlers, they must chase the tree of life to prevent the allied races from seizing it  
* All known organized intelligent life forms vye for position to claim the source of Vitaelum  
* The starlanes of Conway’s Web are filled with space faring life that exist in a food chain of solar energy and dark matter  
  * Herds of Oekigae are massive floating blimp like creatures that are among the largest creatures in the known universe  
  * Massive predators hunt the Oekigae, and pose a substantial threat to individual craft  
  * The threats of space faring creatures slow the exploration of the web  
* At the end of their quest the crew will possess the tree of life, only to find they have been deceived  
  * The tree of life has a twin that hides in its shadow, the tree of knowledge  
  * The tree of knowledge disguises itself and presents itself as the its twin to beguile the pursuers  
  * Once the tree of knowledge has been tasted, the secrets of the universe are laid bare which is too much for mortal comprehension  
  * The crew realizes the tree of life has escaped, and that they possess new powers that defy the laws they once thought defined the universe  
    * There are hints throughout the exploration of the new filament of psychic awakenings and unnatural abilities  
    * These were latent powers awakened by the passing of the tree of knowledge  
    * The interaction with the tree of knowledge sends a ripple effect through the nearby stars, starting an awakening of new psychic abilities across tens of thousands of sentient creatures

## Cairo Gate

You’re in one of the holding yards for Cairo gate, dry docked to platform that connects thousands of ships to a central refueling station. There are barely any amenities at this station, and crews typically stay sealed inside their ships to let drones perform the refueling and life support maintenance outside the ship.

Your destination comes through an encrypted text in one word, *“Pleiades”*

You are to chart your course to the Pleiades cluster.

Orienting yourself in the command center of your ship, you chart your course and prepare for action.

There are several battle stations you may choose from when your vehicle  enters combat

Navigation: A crew member makes a check against their piloting skill against a given difficulty. When evading enemy fire, higher velocity evasive maneuvers will increase the chance of success. This is limited by available fuel and the ability of crew members to endure high G forces. See evasive maneuvers for more details.

\*\*Shields\*\*: A crew member makes an intuition check to predict where the shield should be concentrated for impact. If the check is successful, the shield will absorb all of the incoming energy until the shield or incoming energy is consumed. If the check fails, some of the energy will penetrate the shield and damage the hull of the ship.

Calculations:  
\- Shield overpenetration hull damage

\*\*Comms\*\*: A crew member attempts to hail other ships and makes charisma-based checks to effect the interaction.

\*\*Targeting\*\*: A crew member selects targeting priority and allocated power per weapon on a given turn. This crew member makes luck rolls to see how quickly enemy projectiles can be shot down by  laser turrets each turn.

Calculations:  
\- Laser turret projectile destruction

\*\*Network Defense\*\*: A crew member attempts to penetrate opponent network defense to weaken or immobilize opposing ship functions.

\*\*Maintenance\*\*: A crew member is an space suit inside the maintenance bay making hardware maintenance checks to identify excessive and address excessive heat buildup. This is a dangerous job, and will require strapping in when the ship engages in high G maneuvers.
